## Weight
- measured in API gravity
- created to measure the density of oil (i.e. petroleum liquid’s density relative to that of water)
- (by American Petroleum Institute) - therefore API?
	- **Heavy Oil**
	- evaporates slowly
	- contains material that will be used to make heavy products like asphalt, and feedstock for plastics & petrochemicals.
	- the lower the API, heavier the oil.
